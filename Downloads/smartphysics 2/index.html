A/L Physics full syllabus
Complete coverage of the Sri Lanka A/L Physics syllabus — all units, all topics
📐

1. Measurement

Physical quantities, SI units, dimensions, significant figures, errors and uncertainties, scalar and vector quantities.

Unit 1
🏃

2. Mechanics

Kinematics, Newton's laws of motion, momentum, impulse, work, energy, power, circular motion, gravitation, and simple harmonic motion.

Unit 2
🌡️

3. Thermal Physics

Temperature, heat, specific heat capacity, latent heat, thermal expansion, gas laws, kinetic theory of gases, and thermodynamics.

Unit 3
🌊

4. Waves

Wave motion, transverse and longitudinal waves, superposition, interference, diffraction, standing waves, sound waves, and the Doppler effect.

Unit 4
💡

5. Optics

Reflection, refraction, total internal reflection, lenses and mirrors, optical instruments, wave nature of light, and polarisation.

6. Electricity & Magnetism

Electric fields, Coulomb's law, capacitance, current electricity, Ohm's law, circuits, magnetic fields, electromagnetic induction, and AC theory.

Unit 6

Photoelectric effect, atomic structure, Bohr model, X-rays, radioactivity, nuclear reactions, and fundamental particles.
